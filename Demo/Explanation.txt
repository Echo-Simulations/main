Download Unity 2022.1.23f1
Open a project
Make a simple scene
Drag the Scripts folder into the Unity Assets folder
Drag and drop the Ray Tracing Master.cs script onto the sound source
Drag and drop the Ray Tracing Object.cs script onto all meshes that you want to include in the demo
Tag the listener as "Listener"
Drag and drop TraceAudioRays.compute into the Ray Tracing Master.cs 'Ray Tracing Shader' field
Press play
